Kafe Kombit (FONDATION BLOCS CHAINES COMMUNES) Kafe Kombit présente un instrument de développement appelé 'KOMBIT' (KBT). Kombit est proposé comme devise numerique avec des mécanismes de vote démocratique pour des projets de développement d'affaires communs en Haïti.

In 1821, Greece sent a plea to Haiti for help in their struggles for Independence from the Ottoman Empire rule. Haiti, embattled and still struggling against ransoms since its own Independence, answered the call and sent moral support including 25 tons of coffee. Kafe Kombit: ‘Kombit’ aka 'Konbit' is a Haitian Kreyol term used to define a community working together towards a common goal. Kombit platform Tokens was developed by a Haitian Canadian architectural technologist, and presented by a Finnish visual artist during the Platforms Project in Athens in May 2017. Kombit is proposed as a cryptocurrency backed token with democratic voting to fund development projects in Haiti.
